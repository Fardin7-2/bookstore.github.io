Indigo Awakening

By Hanika Kumar



Kanika has always been afraid of the dark. Even as a mother to an eleven-year-old, she is unable to overcome her fear. Soon, however, Kanika is left with no choice but to face her terror, as the darkness begins to push her towards the edge. Caught between the fear of the dark and the fear of insanity, Kanika decides to face the unknown. As she turns to confront the darkness, the lines between reality and imagination get displaced, throwing Kanika into the mind-bending act of questioning her own sanity.



Thriller,Horror
